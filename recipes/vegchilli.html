Super Simple Vegetarian Chilli

Ingredients

  • Canned Golden Sweet Corn
  • Canned Diced Tomatoes
  • Canned Light Red Kidney Beans
  • Diced Onions
  • Minced Garlic (can substitute Garlic Powder)
  • Powdered Chilli Seasoning Mix

Steps

  1. Add sweet corn, onions, diced tomatoes, and kidney beans to a large pot
  2. Bring bot to a boil
  3. When pot begins to boil, add garlic and chilli seasoning mix to pot and stir
  4. Reduced heat and simmer for about 10 minutes
  5. Carefully taste after simmering to ensure desired results, add more seasoning as needed until desired taste.
  6. Serve in bowl and enjoy
